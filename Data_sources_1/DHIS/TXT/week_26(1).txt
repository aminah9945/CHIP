*EDITORIAL NOTE:* District Health Information System2 (DHIS2) is an online computerized information system in Health Department under which
Daily/monthly reports comprising of information on selected indicators are collected from the health facilities. It is utmost important that information generated
through DHIS2 should be analyzed and be disseminated to all concerned for well-informed based decision making. Based upon the data r eceived from the
health facilities through online system of Multan District a feedback report is developed. This gives a comparison of some essential services related to SDG
(Sustainable Development Goals) rendered through the health facilities in the district. It is hoped that this r elease would help Health Managers in determining
the existing situation of these important services and would be a strong basis for taking timely remedial measures.
