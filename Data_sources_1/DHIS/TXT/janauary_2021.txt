    KEY HIGHLIGHTS

    Due to pandemic COVID-19, there is a clear decline pattern observed from Oct to Dec 2020 in healthcare services
    that are being reported in DHIS by Health facilities including Teaching Hospitals, DHQ, THQ, Civil Hospitals, RHCs,
    BHUs, MCHC and Dispensaries. Total OPD visits in Oct to Dec were 32 Million, but from Oct (12.7 Million) OPD
    is decreased to 4 Million in Dec 2020. This shows there is a decrease of 31% in OPD for the month of Dec as
    compare to Oct 2020.
    Similarly, a major decline is observed in rest of the major indictors that are being reported in DHIS i.e.
    ANC-I, Deliveries, Family planning visits, Medicine Stock out and other indicators.
