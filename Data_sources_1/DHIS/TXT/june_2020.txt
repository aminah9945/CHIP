KEY HIGHLIGHTS
Due to pandemic COVID-19, there is a clearly decline observed in healthcare services that are being reported
in DHIS by Health facilities including Teaching Hospitals, DHQ, THQ, Civil Hospitals, RHCs, BHUs, MCHC
and Dispensaries till May 2020. The OPD (Out Patient Department) of THOS (Teaching Hospitals) were closed
by the end of March. Total OPD visits in January & February were 14 Million, but from March (11.6 Million)
OPD is decreased to 6 Million in May 2020.
In June 2020, it is observed that Rural Settlements have shown a bit decline of 3%, whereas, there is increase
of 8% in OPD visits in urban settlements as compared with May 2020.
Similarly, increased is observed in rest of the major indictors that are being reported in DHIS i.e. ANC-I,
Deliveries, Family planning visits, Medicine Stock out and other indicators for the month of June 2020.
