 <u>Communicable and Non-Communicable Diseases:</u>
 The data from Week 18 in 2025 from Punjab highlighted significant cases of major communicable diseases. Acute Respiratory Infection (AURI) stood out, constituting 56% of
 cases, followed by Diarrhea/Gastro. issues and Scabies at 19% and 9%. Conversely, the highest percentage for Non- Communicable Diseases was Fever due to unknown causes
 at 29%, followed by Hypertension and Diabetes both at 11% respectively. *These percentages were derived from patients visiting the OPD for communicable and non-
