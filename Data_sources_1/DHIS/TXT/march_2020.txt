                                                        March-2020
Monthly Report

In March 2020, overall there is a clearly decline observed due to pandemic COVID-19 in
healthcare services that are being reported in DHIS. Total OPD visits in March 2020 were
13.7M and in February 2020, OPD visits were 14.3 M. Hence, the decline percentage is 5%
in OPD. Similarly, it is mentioned in this report that there’s a decrease in Family planning
visits, ANC-I visits and Deliveries conducted in health facilities, because of COVID-19 and
OPD of THOS were closed by the 23ʳᵈ of March.
