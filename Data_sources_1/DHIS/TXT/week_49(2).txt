    In the 49th week of 2024, there were 47,560 Family Planning visits. Of these, 2% (1,056) were by women under 25, and 66% (31,186) were new clients. Counseling visits

    popular methods included POP cycles (1%), implants and tubal ligation both at 0.30% and 0.13% respectively.
