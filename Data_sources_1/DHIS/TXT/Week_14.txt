 Communicable and Non‐Communicable Diseases:
 The data from Week 14 in 2024 from Punjab highlighted significant cases of major communicable diseases. Acute Upper Respira‐
 tory Infection (AURI) stood out, constituting 67% of cases, followed by Diarrhea/Gastrointestinal issues at 12% and Scabies 10%,
 Conversely, the highest percentage for Non‐Communicable Diseases was Fever due to unknown causes at 34%, followed by Peptic
 Ulcer and Hypertension at 12% and 10%, respectively. *These percentages were derived from patients visiting the OPD for communi‐
