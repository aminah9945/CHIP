**Summary:**

In the weekly report, various indicators of health facilities have been discussed and the data of teaching/tertiary care hospitals are also included. The detailed analysis of week 46 of year 2022 is being presented in this report. The daily reporting regularity of district Vehari is 100% as it fulfils the reporting compliance target for the week 46 of year 2022.

The total OPD Visits during week 46 of 2022 were approximately 2.0 million (2,048,271) out of which 660K (660,425) were reported from BHU’s, 308K (307,809) from RHC’s, 441K (441,177) from THQ hospitals, 222K (222,490) from DHQ(all) and 416K (416,370) were reported from Teaching hospitals.

Sixty diseases were reported through DHIS-2. Antenatal care coverage is an indicator of access and utilization of RMNCH services has been also analyzed.

During week 46, the overall ANC-1 reporting of Punjab was 60,536 against the total expected population (3.4%). Overall, the highest number of visits of ANC-1 have been recorded at BHU’s from district Faisalabad as. 4,174. Delivery coverage at health facility is an indicator of utilization of delivery services provided at public health facilities. During week 46 the highest (46%) C-section cases has reported in district Lahore.
