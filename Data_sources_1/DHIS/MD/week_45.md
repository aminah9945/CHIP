## Highlights:

The detail analysis of week 45 of year 2023 data is presented in this report. The reporting compliance week 45, 2023 of all 36 Districts was 95%. The figure is similar to that of the previous week. The Total OPD Visits during week 45 of 2023 was 19,37,956. OPD visits 18% decreased as compared to the previous week (23,65,803 in week 44 vs 19,37,956 in week 45) while 96% are new OPD patients and only 4% are follow up visits in current week. The average OPD per day visits in Facility types wise was higher in THQS i.e. (886/day visits). The highest number of OPD visits were conducted in District Lahore (1,77,609) followed by Faisalabad (1,67,192) and Multan (1,04,955) during the period of week 45. In age and gender wise analysis the percentage of female (54%) was higher than male (46%) patients and the highest number of patients were reported in (15-49) y age group.

68 diseases were reported through DHIS-2. The patients reported diseases constitute overall 57% of the total patients in week 45, 2023. Out of 68 Priority diseases, (58%) communicable and (43%) are non-communicable disease patients. Top 10 communicable disease presented in the form of graphs and Tables.

Total ANC (1,98,870) visits were recorded in the Punjab, out of total ANC-1 (n= 75,365) women 16% were reported with Hb<10g/dl. Total deliveries 25,676 was conducted in Punjab during current week, 80% Normal deliveries, 20% C-Section and only 0.17% were Vacuum/Forceps. Out of total live births i.e., 25,346, 2% babies were born with LBW <2.5 kg and 0.5% were Premature Births.

Total FP visits (39,469) were recorded in this week. The highest FP visits 77% were recorded in BHU. Highest FP visits were recorded in District Faisalabad (3,676), Multan (2,180) and in Okara (2,072).

Total indoor 1,07,447 patients were admitted during current week, out of which 60% patient disease wise admitted. Out of total Surgeries (18,398) performed by Anesthesia GA (18%), LA (48%), spinal (28%) and 6% other types of Anesthesia.
