**Summary:**

In the weekly report, various indicators of health facilities have been discussed and the data of teaching/tertiary care hospitals are also included. The detailed analysis of week 51 of year 2022 is being presented in this report. The daily reporting regularity of district sahiwal and vehari are 99% (highest among all districts) whereas the reporting compliance target is 100%.

The total OPD Visits during week 51 of 2022 were approximately 2.1 million (2,123,423) out of which 698K (697,831) were reported from BHU’s, 319K (318,558) from RHC’s, 483K (483,183) from THQ hospitals, 219K (219,311) from DHQ(all) and 405K (404,510) were reported from Teaching hospitals.

Sixty diseases were reported through DHIS-2. Antenatal care coverage is an indicator of access and utilization of RMNCH services has been also analyzed.

During week 51, the overall ANC-1 reporting of Punjab was 64,775 against the total expected population (3.4%). Overall, the highest number of visits of ANC-1 have been recorded at BHU’s from district Faisalabad as. 5,334. Delivery coverage at health facility is an indicator of utilization of delivery services provided at public health facilities. During week 50 the highest (46%) C-section cases has reported in district Lahore.
