## Highlights:

The Total OPD Visits during week 44 of 2023 was 22,84,408. 3% OPD visits increased as compared to the previous week (22,14,408 in week 43 vs 22,84,408 in week 44) while 96% are new OPD patients and only 4% are follow up visits in current week. The average OPD per day visits in Facility types wise was higher in THQS i.e. (1161/day visits). The highest number of OPD visits were conducted in District Lahore (2,42,441) followed by Faisalabad (1,98,620) and Multan (1,25,054) during the period of week 44. In age and gender wise analysis the percentage of female (54%) was higher than male (46%) patients and the highest number of patients were reported in (15-49) y age group. The overall reporting compliance in Punjab week 44 was 95%. The figure is similar to that of the previous week.

68 diseases were reported through DHIS-2. the patients reported diseases constitute overall 56% of the total patients in week 44, 2023. Out of 68 Priority diseases, (57%) communicable and (43%) are non-communicable disease patients. Top 10 communicable disease presented in the form of graphs and Tables.

Total ANC (2,11,453) visits were recorded in the Punjab, out of total ANC-1 (n=81,778) women 17% were reported with Hb<10g/dl. Total deliveries 26,449 was conducted in Punjab during current week, 79% Normal deliveries, 21% C-Section and only 0.23% were Vacuum/Forceps. Out of total live births i.e., 26,065, 2% babies were born with LBW <2.5 kg. Total FP visits (41,423) were recorded in this week. The highest FP visits 76% were recorded in BHU.

Total indoor 1,19,155 patients were admitted during current week, out of which 59% patient disease wise admitted. Out of total Surgeries (21,586) performed by anesthesia in which GA (19%), LA (48%), spinal Anesthesia (27%) and 6% other types of Anesthesia.
