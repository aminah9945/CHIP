## Summary:

In this report, different indicators of health facility are discussed. The data of teaching/tertiary care hospitals is also included. The detailed analysis of week 23 of year 2022 are being presented in this report, The reporting regularity of districts Bahawalnagar Multan, Gujrat, Rawalpindi, Sahiwal, Rahimyar Khan, Vehari, Jhelum, T.T Singh and Mianwali are approximately 100% whereas district Hafizabad, Jhang, Okara, Khanewal, Pakpattan, Rajanpur, Muzaffargarh, Layyah, Khusab, Gujranwala, Attock and Sargodha did not meet the reporting compliance for the week 23 of year 2022.

The total OPD Visits during week 23 of 2022 was 1.9M out of which 629K were reported in BHU(all), 320K were reported in RHC (all), 584K were reported in THQ (all), 100K were reported in DHQ(all) and 163K were reported in Teaching hospitals.

On average, 42 OPD per day visits were reported in BHU’s, 311 per day visits were reported in RHC’s, 825 per day visits were reported in THQ hospitals, 1,386 per day OPD visits were reported in DHQ hospitals and 969 OPD per day visits were reported in Teaching Hospitals. It is calculated as total OPD visits by dividing the number of reporting health facilities and the total number of days excluding Sunday.

Fifty-three diseases are reported through DHIS, out of which eleven prone Epidemic diseases of 35 districts of Punjab on which DHIS-2 is implemented successfully, have been discussed in this report through DHIS-2 software.

Antenatal care coverage is an indicator of access and utilization of health care services during pregnancy.

During week 23, the overall ANC-1 reported coverage in 35 districts of Punjab was 71,356 of the total expected population (3.4%). Overall, the highest number of visits of ANC-1 has been recorded in district Faisalabad under the health facility BHU i.e. 2,343 visits.

Delivery coverage at health facility is an indicator of utilization of delivery services provided at public health facilities. The overall percentage of deliveries conducted in health facilities of 31 districts of Punjab. During week 23 the highest (24%) C-section cases has reported in district Rawalpindi and the highest cases (99%) of normal delivery cases were reported in district Lodhran.

Indoor patient’s data of week 19 has shown in (fig. 6 & fig. 7) which cleared the statistics of indoor admissions and death.

During week 23, family planning visits reported from the public health facilities against the expected population were 36, 688. The highest number of FP visits were reported in district Bahawalnagar (83%) under the health facility Basic Health Unit.
