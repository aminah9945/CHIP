## CASE DEFINITION

**Acute Viral Hepatitis {A & E} (Acute Jaundice Syndrome)**
Suspected Case: An acute illness with discrete onset of symptoms of jaundice, dark urine, anorexia, malaise, extreme fatigue, and right upper quadrant tenderness OR elevated serum alanine aminotransferase (ALT) level > 2.5 times the upper limit.
