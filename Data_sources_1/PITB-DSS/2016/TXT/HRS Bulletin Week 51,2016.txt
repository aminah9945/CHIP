 CASE DEFINITION
 Sexually transmitted infections (STls) are infections that are spread primarily through person-to-person sexual
contact. There are more than 30 different sexually transmissible bacteria, viruses and parasites.

              The most common conditions they cause are Gonorrhoea, Chlamydial infection, Syphilis, Trichomoniasis,
Chancroid, Genital Herpes, Genital Warts, Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV/AlDS) Infection and Hepatitis B
Infection.
