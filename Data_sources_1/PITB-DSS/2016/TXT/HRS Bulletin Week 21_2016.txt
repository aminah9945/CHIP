    CASE DEFINITION
    Skin infection characterized by rash or lesions and intense itching especially at night.
    Lesions are prominent around finger webs, wrists, elbows, axillae, beltlines, thighs,
    external genitalia, nipples, abdomen and lower portion of buttocks. Head, neck, palm and
    soles of infants may be involved.
