    CASE DEFINITION
    Suspected Case: Any person in whom a clinician suspects measles infection OR
    Any person with fever, and generalized maculopapular rash (i.e. non vesicular) and one of the
    following: cough, coryza (i.e. runny nose) and conjunctivitis (i.e. red eye).
