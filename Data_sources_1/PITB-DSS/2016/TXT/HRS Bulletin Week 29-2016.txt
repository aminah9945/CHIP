    CASE DEFINITION
    Unexplained Fever: Fever (body temperature >101.5 °F) for >48 hours and without other known etiology.
    Pyrexia of Unknown Origin: A patient having Fever of > 101.5 °F for more than 3 weeks with no established
    diagnosis despite appropriate investigation for 1 week.
