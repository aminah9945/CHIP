     Children ≤ 5 years: Any child presenting with cough or difficult breathing with any one of these:

     12 months to 5 years, ≥ 40 breaths/min).
     OLower chest wall in-drawing  Unable to drink or breastfeed  Difficulty to awaken
     Fits / convulsions Stridor in calm child.
     Over 5 years: Any person presenting with acute onset of cough, fever, difficulty in breathing and
     chest pain which increases with breathing.
