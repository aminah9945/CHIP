## CASE DEFINITION

**Children ≤ 5 years** Any child presenting with cough or difficult breathing with any one of these:

* fast breathing, (Less than 2 months ≥ 60 breaths/min, 2 months to 12 months, ≥50 breaths /min, 12 months to 5 years, ≥ 40 breaths/min).

* Lower chest wall in-drawing
* Unable to drink or breastfeed
* Difficulty to awaken

* Fits / convulsions
* Stridor in calm child.

**Over 5 years** Any person presenting with acute onset of cough, fever, difficulty in breathing and chest pain which increases with breathing.
