# (SUSPECTED) FOCUSING MENINGITIS
(23<sup>rd</sup> FEB, 2014 TO 28<sup>th</sup> FEB, 2015)
TOTAL ENTRIES = 566

## CASE DEFINITION

Suspected Case: An acute illness with sudden onset of high grade fever (>38.5 °C rectal and >38 °C axillary) and one or more of the following:

* Neck stiffness

* Altered consciousness

* Other meningeal signs

* Petechial/purpural rashes

In patients under one year of age, when fever is accompanied by bulging fontanelle.

Geographical map showing dengue activity locations

Smart phone based dengue surveillance continues in week 9. Rawalpindi, Gujranwala, Sialkot, Sargodha, Faisalabad, Hafizabad, Lahore, Sahiwal, Okara, Multan, Muzaffargarh, Rajanpur and Rahim Yar Khan have carried out activities pertaining to anti dengue activities as per SOP's of the government.
