## CASE DEFINITION

Suspected Case of Tuberculosis: Any person who presents with symptoms or sign suggestive of Pulmonary Tuberculosis, in particular cough of long duration. Symptoms may also include hemoptysis, chest pain, breathlessness, fever/night sweats, tiredness, loss of appetite and significant weight loss.
