 CASE DEFINITION
 S
Suspected case A person with a cough lasting at least 2 weeks with one of the following:
Paroxysms i.e. bouts of coughing or inspiratory "whoop" or post-tussive vomiting (i.e. vomiting immediately after
coughing) and without other apparent cause.
Or
 A case diagnosed as pertussis by a physician.
