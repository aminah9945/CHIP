CASE DEFINITION
Suspected Case:
Cutaneous Leishmaniasis (Saldana/Kaldana): Appearance of one or more skin lesions, typically on uncovered
parts of body (Face, Neck, Arms and Legs) which begin as nodules and turn into skin ulcers eventually healing
 but leaving a depressed Scar.
