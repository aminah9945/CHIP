CASE DEFINITION
Suspected Case: Patient with sudden onset of illness with high grade fever (>38.5°C) for > 72 hours and
<10 days especially in CCHF endemic areas and among those in contact with a confirmed patient or
handling animals and raw animal products AND fever usually associated with headache & muscle pain and
does not respond to antibiotics or anti-malarial treatment.
