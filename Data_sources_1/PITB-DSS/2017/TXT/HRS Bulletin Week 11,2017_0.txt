CASE DEFINITION
Suspected Case: Not applicable
Probable case: An acute illness characterized by an adherent membrane on the tonsils, pharynx
and/or nose and one of the following: laryngitis, pharyngitis or tonsillitis.
Confirmed Case: A confirmed case is a probable case that has been confirmed through
laboratory investigation or linked epidemiologically to a laboratory confirmed case.
