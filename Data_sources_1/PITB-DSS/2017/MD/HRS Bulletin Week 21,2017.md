## CASE DEFINITION

**Suspected Case: Unexplained Fever:** Fever (body temperature >101.5 °F) for >48 hours and without other known etiology.

**Pyrexia of Unknown Origin:** A Patient having Fever of > 101.5 °F for more than 3 weeks with no established diagnosis despite appropriate investigations for 1 week.
