## CASE DEFINITION

Suspected cholera case:

\* In an area where the disease is not known to be present: Severe dehydration or death from acute watery diarrhea in a patient aged 5 years or more.

\* In an area where cholera is endemic: Acute watery diarrhea, with or without vomiting in a patient aged 5 years or more.

\* In an area where there is a cholera epidemic: Acute watery diarrhea, with or without vomiting, in any patient.
