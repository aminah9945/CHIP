Public Health Actions


1. Acute Diarrhea (Non-Cholera)

   x     Community Education: Launch health education campaigns on hand washing, food hygiene, and safe
         drinking water.
   x     Field Verification: Health workers should verify suspected cases and report them immediately.
   x     Strengthen Surveillance: Quickly enhance reporting systems for acute diarrhea and suspected cholera
         cases, particularly in high-risk areas.



2. Malaria

x     Expand Distribution of Insecticide-Treated Nets (ITNs): Distribute ITNs and ensure their use, particularly in
      malaria-endemic areas.
x     Strengthen Case Management: Ensure malaria diagnosis and treatment are available, particularly at the
      community level.
x     Public Awareness: Run health education and community awareness campaigns, especially targeting high-
      risk groups.
x     Improve Malaria Surveillance: Strengthen malaria case surveillance and data reporting systems to identify
      and manage outbreaks.
