Infographic about Mpox in Urdu language showing symptoms and prevention

## Highlights

* The overall reporting compliance rate stands at 99.6 %, indicating strong adherence.
* During Epi week 18, the most frequently reported cases were of Acute Diarrhea (Non-Cholera) followed by ILI, Pneumonia/ALRI, Tuberculosis, Animal / Dog Bite and Severe Acute Respiratory Infection.
* Among Water / Food Borne Diseases, Acute Diarrhea (Non-Cholera) cases have decreased this week.
* No death reported during this week
* 04 cases of Acute Flaccid Paralysis reported this week.
* 112 cases of Animal / Dog Bite reported during Epi week 18.
* 05 cases of chickenpox / Varicella have been reported during this week.
* This week, 112 tuberculosis cases have been reported (58 cases from district Poonch)

Photograph of a training session in a conference room

A comprehensive training session on “Disease Surveillance & Levels of Prevention” was conducted for LHS's and Sector Incharges by IDSRS Team district Haveli.

Photograph of an awareness workshop with participants seated at a table

The DHO Office, Bagh conducted an awareness workshop on dengue and malaria for LHS and CDC staff. Teams IDSRS district Bag also participated in said activity.
