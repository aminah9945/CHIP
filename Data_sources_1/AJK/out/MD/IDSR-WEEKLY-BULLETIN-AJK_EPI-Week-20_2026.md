## Highlights

* The overall reporting compliance rate stands at 99.6 %, indicating strong adherence.

* During Epi week 20, the most frequently reported cases were of Acute Diarrhea (Non-Cholera) followed by ILI, Pneumonia/ALRI, Severe Acute Respiratory Infection, Animal / Dog Bite and Tuberculosis.

* Among Water / Food Borne Diseases, Acute Diarrhea (Non-Cholera) cases have increased this week.

* No death reported during this week.

* 01 case of HIV/AIDS reported from district Muzaffarabad.

* 08 cases of Acute Flaccid Paralysis (05 from Mirpur, 02 from MZD and 01 from Bagh) have been reported during this week.

* 113 cases of Animal / Dog Bite reported during Epi week 20.

* 101 Tuberculosis cases reported during this Epi week .

Photographs showing a meeting with DG Health AJ&K and the delivery of laboratory equipment

A technical team from IDSR-NIH visited the DG Health Office Muzaffarabad to brief the Honorable DG Health, AJ&K, regarding the laboratory equipment and consumables provided by IDSRS-NIH, MoNHSR&C Islamabad to Abbas Institute of Medical Sciences (AIMS) Hospital Muzaffarabad.
